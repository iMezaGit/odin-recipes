Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

Makes one pizza for four

For the dough:

  • 500g Tipo 00 flour
  • 350 cL water
  • 10g yeast (approximately one tablespoon)
  • 20g salt
  • A little olive oil

For the base:

  • 250g tomatoes (mixed with a pinch of salt, a little olive oil, and basil or oregano)

Preparation

First, mix 500g of Tipo 00 flour with 350 cL of water in a large bowl.
Add 10g of yeast (approximately one tablespoon) and sprinkle in 20g of salt.
Drizzle in a little olive oil for flavor and texture.
Next, knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10-15 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
Allow it to rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.
Preheat your oven to the maximum temperature (ideally around 300°C / 572°F).
Punch down the risen dough to release any air bubbles, then shape it into a round or rectangular pizza base.
Mix 250g of tomatoes with a pinch of salt, a drizzle of olive oil, and some fresh basil or oregano,
and spread this tomato mixture evenly over the pizza base. Partially bake the base for 4-5 minutes to prevent a soggy crust.
After the initial bake, add 250g of mozzarella (or more, if you like it cheesy) and other toppings of your choice—ham,
spicy salami, tuna, anchovies, etc. Return the pizza to the oven and bake until the cheese is
melted, bubbly, and golden brown, and the crust is fully cooked. Slice your homemade pizza and savor the deliciousness!
Buon appetito!
